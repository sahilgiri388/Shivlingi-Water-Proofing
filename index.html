Our Services

Comprehensive Waterproofing & Construction Services

From rooftops to basements, tunnels to terraces — we provide end-to-end waterproofing and construction solutions for every structure.

💧

Waterproofing

Comprehensive waterproofing for rooftops, terraces, basements, and wet areas using modern techniques and proven materials.

🏗️

Civil Work

Full-scale civil construction and repair carried out by trained engineers and supervisors ensuring structural integrity.

🧪

Chemical Coating

Advanced chemical coatings providing superior protection against water ingress, corrosion, and environmental degradation.

🔩

Grouting

Precision grouting to fill cracks, voids, and joints in concrete and masonry structures for lasting water resistance.

✨

Silicone Coating

High-performance silicone sealant applications for glass, masonry, joints, and facades providing weather resistance.

🏠

Roof Sealant

Professional roof sealant application that prevents water penetration and extends the lifespan of roof structures.

🌡️

Roof Insulation

Thermal and waterproof roof insulation systems that reduce heat gain, prevent moisture, and improve energy efficiency.

🪨

Stone Waterproofing

Specialized treatments for natural and engineered stone surfaces, preserving aesthetics while preventing water damage.

🔵

PU Coating

Polyurethane liquid waterproofing coatings offering seamless, flexible, and highly durable protection for all surfaces.

💡

PU Grouting

Polyurethane injection grouting for structural crack repair, waterproofing, and stabilization in concrete structures.

📦

APP Membrane

Atactic Polypropylene modified bitumen membrane for heavy-duty waterproofing on roofs and foundations.

🎨

POP & Painting

Plaster of Paris finishing and premium painting services for interior and exterior walls with smooth, durable results.

🧱

Brick Bat Coba

Traditional brick bat coba waterproofing treatment for terraces and flat roofs — time-tested protection for Indian structures.
